Introduction

I am a Mechanical Engineer and researcher focused on structural integrity, fracture mechanics, fatigue behavior, mechanical design, and simulation-driven engineering. My work combines finite element analysis, experimental validation, and machine learning to solve problems in pipeline systems, automotive structures, and advanced materials.

My background includes research and industry experience in Abaqus, ANSYS, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, LS-DYNA, and Python, with applications in fracture, fatigue, vibration, manufacturability, and reliability assessment.
