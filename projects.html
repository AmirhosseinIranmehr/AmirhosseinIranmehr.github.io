Selected Projects

Below are some of the main research and engineering projects I have worked on.

Pipeline Failure Data Processing and Root-Cause Analysis

Processed historical accident data to investigate mechanical and environmental routes of failure and identify the main parameters affecting pipeline incidents.

Focus: data analysis, engineering interpretation, failure trends

Tensile Strain Capacity of Spiral Welded Pipes

Developed XFEM-based models to predict the tensile strain capacity and fracture behavior of spiral welded pipes and X70 pipeline steel under mixed-mode loading.

Tools: Abaqus, XFEM, fracture mechanics methods

Focus: crack behavior, mixed-mode loading, structural integrity

Fatigue Analysis of Welded Components Using Surrogate LSTM-XFEM Models

Built a surrogate deep learning and XFEM workflow for fatigue crack growth prediction in welded pipelines. Generated fatigue datasets using parameters such as stress ratio, material zone, and stress range.

Tools: Abaqus, XFEM, Python, machine learning

Focus: fatigue crack growth, future prediction, data-driven modeling

Damage and Structural Analysis of 3D-Printed / Composite Structures

Investigated mechanical performance and damage behavior of advanced structures using finite element analysis and design optimization methods.

Tools: Abaqus, NX, Rhino/Grasshopper

Mechanical Design for Manufacturing Applications

Designed industrial machinery and mechanical parts with emphasis on functionality, manufacturability, and structural reliability.

Tools: SolidWorks, AutoCAD, CATIA
